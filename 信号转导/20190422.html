诱导系统在遗传学上的应用

什么事诱导系统

Based on inducible genes whose expressioin is turned on by the presence of some substance.

Presence:

  • physicla

Why do we need inducible system?

Application of inducible system

Basic research

  • 基因产物的表达（影响生长等）
  • 基因产物的表达（不同生长阶段的）
  • 不同级别的影响的区分
  • 初级影响本身的分析
  • 区分不同的影响

Biotechnological applications

  • Construction of a conditional male sterility system
  • Expression of transgenes that interfere with regeneration, growth or reproduction
  • Conditional expression of resistance genes as a means of pest management to delay adaptive processes of the pathogen
  • Simultaneous induction of processes such as flowering and leaf abscission

The strategy

Properties required for an ideal inducer

  • High specificity for the transgene, no toxicity
  • High environmental compatibility
  • Convenient application
  • High efficiency at low concentrations and low use rates
  • Low costs
  • Availability of different derivatives with different properties

Properties required for gene/promoter

  • Tied control/low background
  • Well studied

Inducible system of eucaryote

有的时候细胞会降解过表达的蛋白质，因此过表达基因后不一定能够看到表型。利用诱导系统诱导后立刻观察，比如利用confocal观察GFP标记的蛋白质，可以在细胞尚未来得及降解蛋白质时观察到蛋白质的定位等。

有的基因缺失不育（如不开花），可以使用诱导系统使其在不同时期分别表达或不表达。
